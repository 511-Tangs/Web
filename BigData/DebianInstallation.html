Audio Failure

Purge Pulseaudio

Between December 2012 and February 2013 the standard raspbian jessie distribution, and apt-get upgrade, included pulseaudio. Forum posts suggest that pulseaudio can break alsa. Whilst some members identify various workarounds, others find only removal of pulseaudio restores sound output. 

 $ sudo apt-get --purge remove pulseaudio

After that, reboot the system, we recover sound. Then, using "sudo synaptic" to further get rid of pulse related packages, except two libpulse* library packages, since remove them would also remove gdm3, etc.
